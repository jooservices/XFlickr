# Code of Conduct

XFlickr contributors are expected to keep collaboration respectful, focused, and safe.

## Expected Behavior

- Be clear and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still reviewing carefully.
- Keep feedback about code, docs, tests, and operational risk.
- Respect privacy and do not expose secrets or sensitive data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information or credentials.
- Disruptive behavior that blocks project maintenance.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to [admin@jooservices.com](mailto:admin@jooservices.com).
